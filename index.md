# Privacy Policy for CardVault

Effective Date: [Enter Date]

CardVault ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data.

## 1. Information We Access

With your explicit consent, our app may access:

* Gmail email data (read-only access)
* Credit card billing emails from banks

## 2. How We Use Information

We use the accessed data only to:

* Identify credit card bill due dates
* Extract billing amount and relevant details
* Send reminders and notifications to avoid missed payments

## 3. Data Storage

* We do NOT store full email content permanently
* Only essential extracted data (due date, amount) may be stored securely
* Data is never sold or shared with third parties

## 4. Permissions

We request Gmail read-only access solely for reading relevant billing emails. We do NOT:

* Send emails
* Modify emails
* Delete emails

## 5. Data Security

We use secure methods and Firebase services to protect user data.

## 6. User Control

Users can:

* Revoke Gmail access anytime from their Google Account
* Stop using the app and delete their data

## 7. Third-party Services

We may use:

* Google Firebase (for notifications and storage)

We may update this policy. Users will be notified of significant changes.
